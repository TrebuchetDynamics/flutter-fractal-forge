# Privacy Policy — Flutter Fractals

**Effective Date:** February 13, 2026
**Developer:** ComplexChaos
**Package:** com.trebuchetdynamics.fractal.forge

## Summary

Flutter Fractals does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

## Data Collection

**We collect no data.** Specifically:

- No personal information (name, email, phone, location)
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No crash reports sent to external servers
- No advertising identifiers
- No cookies or tracking pixels
- No network requests of any kind

## Data Storage

All data created by the app stays on your device:

- Saved presets are stored locally using Android SharedPreferences
- Exported images are saved to your device's storage when you explicitly choose to save or share them
- Exploration history is stored locally only
- Crash logs (debug builds only) are written to local storage and never transmitted

## Permissions

- **Storage / Photos access (if requested by the OS):** Used only when you explicitly save exported images to your device.

## Third-Party Services

Flutter Fractals uses no third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. There are no ads, no analytics providers, and no cloud services.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children. It is suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL. Since no data is collected, changes would only reflect new features.
