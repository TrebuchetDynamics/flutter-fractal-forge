Fractal Forge is a fast, private fractal explorer for artists, learners, educators, and anyone curious about mathematical patterns.

Explore 1,585 production fractals across classic and modern families:
• Mandelbrot, Julia, Burning Ship, Tricorn, Celtic, Buffalo, Nova, Phoenix, and Lyapunov maps
• Newton, Halley, Householder, Schroeder, and other root-finding basins
• Strange attractors, cellular automata, space-filling curves, tilings, and IFS systems
• 3D ray-marched worlds such as Mandelbulb, Mandelbox, and quaternion-style fractals

Fractal Forge renders with GPU-powered GLSL shaders where supported, with precision fallback paths for deeper exploration. Use curated presets to jump to rich regions, adjust iterations, palettes, color styles, and visual parameters, then export high-resolution PNG images for wallpaper, sharing, or study.

Features:
• 1,585 production fractal modules
• Deep zoom and pan controls
• 60+ color schemes with smooth coloring, orbit traps, distance estimation, normal-map relief, and more
• Dual Mandelbrot / Julia viewer
• Auto-explore mode for discovering interesting regions
• Randomizer for instant new looks
• Looper for animated parameter motion
• Fractal Music generation from visual scans
• Wallpaper apply/save tools
• Camera overlay experiments that stay on device
• Export and share tools
• Accessibility support with high contrast, reduced motion, labels, and configurable touch targets

Private by design: no ads, no tracking, no account required, and no data collection.

Use Fractal Forge to create mathematical art, study nonlinear systems, teach fractals, or relax with infinite patterns.
