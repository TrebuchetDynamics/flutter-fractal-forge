Fractal Forge is a fast, private fractal explorer for creating generative math art, fractal wallpapers, and visual studies. Explore 977 production fractals with GPU-accelerated GLSL shaders where supported, deep zoom, and real-time controls.

Discover classic and modern fractal families:
• Mandelbrot and Julia sets, Burning Ship, Tricorn, Celtic, Buffalo, Nova, Phoenix, and Lyapunov maps
• Newton, Halley, Householder, Schroeder, and other root-finding basins
• Strange attractors, cellular automata, space-filling curves, tilings, and IFS systems
• 3D ray-marched worlds such as Mandelbulb, Mandelbox, and quaternion-style fractals

Use curated presets to jump to rich regions, adjust iterations, palettes, color styles, orbit traps, distance estimation, normal-map relief, and visual parameters, then export high-resolution PNG images for wallpaper, sharing, teaching, or study.

Features:
• 977 production fractal modules
• GPU-powered rendering where supported
• Deep zoom and pan controls
• 60+ color schemes with smooth coloring
• Dual Mandelbrot / Julia viewer
• Auto-explore mode for discovering interesting regions
• Randomizer for instant new looks
• Looper for animated parameter motion
• Fractal Music generation from visual scans
• Wallpaper apply/save tools
• Camera overlay experiments that stay on device
• Export and share tools
• Accessibility support with high contrast, reduced motion, labels, and configurable touch targets

Private by design: no ads, no tracking, no account required, and no data collection.

Use Fractal Forge to create fractal art, study nonlinear systems, teach math patterns, make unique wallpapers, or relax with infinite visual worlds.
