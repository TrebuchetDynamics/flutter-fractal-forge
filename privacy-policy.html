Privacy Policy

Flutter Fractals

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

This app operates with a privacy-first philosophy: we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data about you. All processing happens on your device.

2. Information We Collect

Short answer: We don't collect any personal information.

What We Don't Collect:

  • No personal data: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any identifying information
  • No analytics: We do not use Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics, or any third-party analytics services
  • No tracking: We do not track your app usage, behavior, or preferences
  • No advertising data: We do not collect data for advertising purposes
  • No network requests for data collection: The app does not send data to external servers for collection or analysis

Transparency Notice: Flutter Fractals is completely ad-free and does not require user accounts, login, or authentication of any kind.

3. Permissions and On-Device Processing

This release does not use camera-based or augmented reality features.

Permissions Used by the App:

  • No camera access: Flutter Fractals does not request camera permission in this release
  • No microphone access: The app does not record audio
  • Optional export access only: On some platforms, the operating system may ask for permission when you explicitly save an exported image to your device library

Processing and Privacy:

  • On-device rendering: Fractals are rendered locally on your device
  • No transmission: Rendered content and exports are not transmitted to external servers or third parties by the app
  • User initiated saves only: Images are written only when you explicitly choose to export them

Key Point: Flutter Fractals renders and stores content locally. The app does not collect or upload your creations.

4. Local Storage

Flutter Fractals stores data locally on your device only. No data is sent to external servers.

What Is Stored Locally:

  • User Presets: Fractal parameters and settings you create and save are stored locally using Android SharedPreferences. These are stored only on your device.
  • Exports: Any images you export are stored in your device's local file system. You have full control over these files and can delete them at any time.
  • App Configuration: Basic app preferences and UI settings are stored locally

Local Storage Privacy:

  • All local data remains on your device under your control
  • Exported images are saved to your device's media library or storage location of your choice
  • No backup is automatically sent to cloud services or external servers
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's cache/storage through device settings

5. Internet Access and Connectivity

Core functionality does not require internet. Flutter Fractals is designed to work completely offline. The app does not require an active internet connection to:

  • Render fractals
  • Explore fractal parameters
  • Save presets and exports

The app operates entirely on your device with no external server dependencies for its core features.

6. Third-Party Services

Flutter Fractals does not use third-party services for data collection or sharing.

No Third-Party Data Sharing:

  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not integrate with advertising networks
  • We do not use social media SDKs that collect data
  • We do not use Firebase or other analytics platforms
  • We do not embed tracking libraries

7. Children's Privacy

Flutter Fractals does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). The app is designed for all ages but does not target children specifically.

Since Flutter Fractals does not collect any personal information from any user regardless of age, children's privacy is fully protected by default. If you have concerns about your child's use of this app, you can:

  • Review the app's functionality on your device
  • Manage permissions through device settings
  • Uninstall the app at any time

8. Security

Since Flutter Fractals does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers, the primary security concern is the protection of files stored locally on your device.

To keep your device and data secure:

  • Keep your Android device updated with the latest security patches
  • Use a strong lock screen PIN or biometric authentication on your device
  • Review app permissions regularly through device settings
  • Download the app only from official sources (Google Play Store)
