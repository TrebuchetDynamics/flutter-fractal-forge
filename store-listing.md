## 2. Full Description (max 4000 characters)

Welcome to Flutter Fractals – a stunning, free app for exploring the infinite beauty of mathematical fractals in real-time.

### What You Can Do

Dive into mesmerizing fractals rendered with high-performance GLSL shaders. Watch as complex mathematical shapes unfold before your eyes with smooth, responsive controls. Whether you're a casual explorer or a serious creator, Flutter Fractals offers tools for everyone.

### Fractal Types

Explore four distinct fractal dimensions:

- **Mandelbrot** - The classic 2D fractal with infinite detail and beautiful self-similarity
- **Julia Set** - 2D fractals with dazzling color patterns and complex structures
- **Burning Ship** - A fiery 2D variant with unique burning-like formations
- **Mandelbulb** - The iconic 3D fractal where you can rotate and zoom through three-dimensional space

### Interactive Controls

Gesture-based exploration designed for touch devices:

- **Drag** to pan across 2D fractals or rotate 3D fractals
- **Pinch to zoom** and explore deeper into the fractal's infinite detail
- **Two-finger rotate** to adjust your perspective in 3D modes
- **Parameter sliders** for real-time customization of power, iterations, zoom, and more

### Presets and Randomization

- Built-in presets for instant inspiration – over 5 curated presets per fractal
- **Randomize** feature to generate stunning new variations with one tap
- Save your own custom presets and return to them anytime
- Parameter locking to randomize specific aspects while keeping others fixed

### Powerful Export Options

Create and share your discoveries:

- Export high-resolution PNG images (up to 4K)
- **Transparent PNG support** for seamless use in designs and projects
- Capture **AR videos** (up to 15 seconds) with your fractals overlaid on the real world
- Share directly to social media, messaging apps, or save to your device

### Augmented Reality (AR) Mode

Transform your surroundings with fractals:

- Camera preview with fractal overlay
- Drag, pinch, and rotate your fractals in real space
- Adjust opacity for perfect blending
- Export AR videos showing your fractals in action
- No account needed – everything stays on your device

### Why You'll Love It

- **No ads, no tracking** – completely privacy-friendly
- **Completely free** – no premium features or paywalls
- **Offline-first** – works without internet connection
- **Bilingual interface** – English and Spanish support
- **Real-time rendering** – smooth 60 FPS exploration
- **No account required** – just open and explore

### Perfect For

- Math enthusiasts exploring the beauty of fractals
- Artists seeking inspiration and unique visuals
- Creators generating stunning background imagery
- Students learning about mathematics and algorithms
- Anyone who loves exploring infinite patterns

Download Flutter Fractals today and begin your journey into the infinite world of fractals.
