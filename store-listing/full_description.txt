Flutter Fractal Forge is the only fractal explorer that lets you place living fractals onto the real world using your camera and ARCore — point your phone at a surface and watch Mandelbrot, Julia, Burning Ship, and more come to life in augmented reality.

Augmented Reality overlay
Use your camera to place fractal overlays on floors, tables, and walls. Tap any surface detected by ARCore to anchor a fractal in your real environment. No audio captured. No data leaves your device.

20+ fractal types rendered by the GPU
Mandelbrot, Julia sets, Burning Ship, Tricorn, Newton basins, Mandelbox 3D, Mandelbulb 3D, Phoenix, Sinh-Julia, and many more — all rendered in real time by fragment shaders for smooth, responsive zoom.

Deep zoom with double-precision
A double-float shader engine provides up to 10¹⁴× magnification before floating-point breakdown — far beyond standard 32-bit rendering.

Dual Mandelbrot / Julia viewer
See Mandelbrot and its corresponding Julia set side by side. Tap any point in the Mandelbrot half to instantly update the Julia seed.

Smooth colour science
sRGB-correct rendering, smooth colouring (continuous iteration count), orbit traps, and an optional glow post-processing effect.

Export your discoveries
Save any fractal as a PNG or animated GIF and share directly from the app.

50+ built-in presets
Jump straight to famous regions — Seahorse Valley, Elephant Valley, deep spiral arms, Julia islands, and more.

No ads. No data collection.
The camera is used only for AR surface detection. No images or personal data are collected or transmitted.
