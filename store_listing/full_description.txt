Flutter Fractal Forge is a high-performance fractal explorer built for deep zoom discovery and creative experimentation. Navigate mathematically rich worlds with fluid gestures, responsive rendering, and a robust fallback architecture designed for reliability across Android devices.

What you can do:
- Explore classic and advanced fractal sets with interactive pan and zoom.
- Use polished touch gestures for precise navigation and fast framing.
- Switch rendering behavior with backend controls (Auto / CPU-only / GPU-only debug mode).
- Recover automatically with CPU fallback if GPU health checks fail.
- Inspect rendering behavior with built-in diagnostics and export logs for troubleshooting.

Why it feels good to use:
- Gesture interactions are tuned for smooth, predictable movement.
- Rendering pipeline is optimized for stability and consistent output.
- App architecture is designed to keep the experience responsive under load.

Accessibility and reliability:
- The app is developed with automation-first validation and measurable quality gates.
- Core rendering quality, performance checks, and viewer behavior are continuously tested.

Whether you are curious about fractal math, looking for mesmerizing visuals, or validating rendering systems, Flutter Fractal Forge gives you a practical and powerful workspace for fractal exploration on Android.
