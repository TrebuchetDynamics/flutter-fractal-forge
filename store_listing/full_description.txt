Fractal Forge is a real-time fractal explorer for browsing, customizing, and exporting mathematical fractals on your device.

Explore 370+ fractal modules across 2D and 3D catalogs. Browse Mandelbrot, Julia, Burning Ship, Phoenix, Newton-style fractals, strange attractors, geometric constructions, cellular systems, and ray-marched 3D forms like Mandelbulb and Mandelbox.

What you can do:
- Browse a large fractal catalog with search, category browsing, dimension filters, and grid/list views
- Navigate with touch gestures: pan and zoom in 2D, orbit and zoom in 3D
- Adjust fractal parameters with responsive controls
- Randomize settings and save your favorite presets
- Export high-resolution PNG images, including transparent PNG when supported
- Share exported still images directly from the app

Built for exploration:
- GPU-first rendering with compatibility fallbacks
- English and Spanish localization
- Accessibility features including screen-reader labels, reduced motion support, and high-contrast options

Privacy-first:
- No accounts
- No ads
- No tracking
- No analytics
- Core exploration works offline

Fractal Forge is designed for curious learners, math enthusiasts, artists, and anyone who enjoys discovering intricate visual patterns.
