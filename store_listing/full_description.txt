Fractal Forge is the most comprehensive fractal explorer on mobile — with 350+ GPU-accelerated fractal types and the ability to place living fractals onto real-world surfaces using your camera and camera.

350+ fractal types — the largest collection on any mobile app
Mandelbrot, Julia sets, Burning Ship, Tricorn, Celtic, Buffalo, Newton basins, Nova, Phoenix, Lyapunov, Buddhabrot and hundreds more — spanning escape-time fractals, strange attractors, cellular automata, space-filling curves, tilings, root-finding methods, and 3D ray-marched fractals (Mandelbulb, Mandelbox). All rendered in real time by GLSL fragment shaders on the GPU.

Use your camera to place fractal overlays on floors, tables, and walls. Tap any surface detected by camera to anchor a fractal in your real environment. No audio captured. No data leaves your device.

Deep zoom with multi-precision rendering
A three-tier precision engine provides float32 GPU rendering, double-float GPU emulation (up to 10^14x magnification), and CPU fallback with full double-precision math — far beyond standard 32-bit rendering.

60+ colour schemes with sRGB-correct science
Smooth colouring (continuous iteration count), orbit traps, normal-map bas-relief shading, distance estimation, stripe averaging, triangle inequality averaging, and curvature averaging techniques. sRGB-correct gamma for accurate colour reproduction.

Dual Mandelbrot / Julia viewer
See Mandelbrot and its corresponding Julia set side by side. Tap any point in the Mandelbrot half to instantly update the Julia seed.

Export and share your discoveries
Save any fractal as a high-resolution PNG with optional transparency and share directly from the app.

Auto-explore mode
Let the app discover interesting regions automatically with intelligent zoom navigation that finds beautiful structures in any fractal.

50+ built-in presets
Jump straight to famous regions — Seahorse Valley, Elephant Valley, deep spiral arms, Julia islands, and more.

Accessibility built in
WCAG AAA high-contrast theme, reduced-motion support, screen reader labels, and configurable touch targets.

No ads. No data collection. No tracking.
The camera is used only for surface detection. No images or personal data are collected or transmitted. Your exploration is entirely private.
