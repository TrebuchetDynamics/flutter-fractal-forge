# Privacy Policy — Flutter Fractal Forge

Last updated: 2026-02-17

Flutter Fractal Forge is designed as an offline fractal rendering application.

## Data Collection
We do **not** collect, store, or share personal data.

Specifically, this app does **not** collect:
- Name, email, phone number, address, or contacts
- Precise or approximate location
- Advertising identifiers for profiling
- Analytics events linked to personal identity
- Payment or financial information

## Network Usage
The app does not require an account and does not depend on remote servers for core fractal rendering.

## Advertising and Monetization
- No third-party ad SDKs are used.
- No in-app purchases are used in this release.

## Children
The app content is mathematical/educational visual content. No social interaction features are provided.

## Permissions
Current release is configured without runtime Android data-access permissions for camera/media/storage.

## Data Security
Because no personal data is collected by the app, there is no personal profile database maintained by the app.

## Changes to this Policy
If data handling changes in future releases, this policy will be updated before rollout.
